# Rubric: investment_memo

**Domain:** investment_memo
**Total Points:** 44
**Pass Threshold:** 0.85

## Criterion 1: memo_structure
**Category:** format
**Max Points:** N/A
**Description:** Document follows standard 1-page memo format with substantive content in all eight required sections
**Pass Condition:** Contains all 8 sections with at least 3 sentences of substantive content per section AND demonstrates interconnected analysis (e.g., team background directly supports technology claims, market opportunity aligns with specific product capabilities). Total word count 600-700 words with no filler content.

## Criterion 2: memo_market
**Category:** analysis
**Max Points:** N/A
**Description:** Market sizing is specific to defense drones with realistic addressable market calculations and relevant industry catalysts
**Pass Condition:** Provides SAM/SOM figures (not just TAM) with either cited sources or clear methodology. Numbers are defense drone-specific, not generic defense/aerospace. Identifies at least 2 specific tailwinds from: DoD budget allocations, recent conflicts/lessons learned, regulatory changes, or technology shifts.

## Criterion 3: memo_thesis
**Category:** conviction
**Max Points:** N/A
**Description:** Investment thesis clearly articulates company-specific competitive advantages and timing rationale
**Pass Condition:** Thesis is 2-3 sentences addressing: (1) specific technological or strategic insight, (2) team's unique qualifications/background, (3) concrete timing catalyst. Mentions specific capabilities, contracts, or advantages that differentiate from other defense drone companies.

## Criterion 4: memo_risks
**Category:** diligence
**Max Points:** N/A
**Description:** Key risks are honest, specific, and include mitigants
**Pass Condition:** 4-5 specific, non-obvious risks with quantified impact estimates where possible. Must include: technology/development risk, regulatory/ITAR risk, customer concentration risk, and one market-specific risk. Each risk has a concrete mitigant with timeline and responsible party identified.

## Criterion 5: memo_deal_terms
**Category:** practicality
**Max Points:** N/A
**Description:** Deal terms are realistic and internally consistent
**Pass Condition:** Pre-money valuation with comparable company justification, round size, lead investor type with rationale for fit, liquidation preferences, board composition, and use of funds broken down by category with specific timelines and milestones. Values must be stage-appropriate (Series A defense: $15-40M pre).
