# Rubric: forensic_accounting_revenue_fraud

**Task:** Prepare a comprehensive forensic accounting analysis of a mid-size company suspected of revenue recognition fraud. The analysis must include ALL of the following components with professional rigor:

1. Benford's Law analysis with statistical validation (chi-square, runs test, MAD)
2. Journal entry testing methodology using AICPA risk-based targeting
3. Revenue recognition scheme detection (channel stuffing, bill-and-hold, round-tripping, side agreements, premature recognition)
4. Analytical procedures with quantified materiality thresholds and trend analysis
5. Digital forensics integration with proper data extraction and preservation
6. Documentary evidence chain with chain of custody procedures
7. Internal control assessment with segregation of duties and management override evaluation
8. Interview and inquiry procedures — structured interviews of key personnel (CFO, controller, sales management) with documentation of responses and inconsistencies
9. Fraud triangle assessment — evaluation of opportunity, pressure/incentive, and rationalization factors specific to the company
10. Related party transaction analysis — identification and testing of revenue transactions with related entities
11. Customer confirmation procedures — independent verification of revenue with top customers and unusual accounts
12. Financial statement restatement impact quantification — calculating the effect of identified misstatements on each affected financial statement line item and key ratios
13. Timeline/chronology construction — building a detailed fraud timeline correlating accounting entries, communications, and external events
14. SSFS (Statement on Standards for Forensic Services) compliance — ensuring all procedures and reporting meet AICPA SSFS No. 1 requirements
15. Threshold avoidance pattern analysis — testing for systematic patterns around approval limits, reporting thresholds, and materiality boundaries

The deliverable is a findings memo suitable for litigation support and expert witness testimony.
